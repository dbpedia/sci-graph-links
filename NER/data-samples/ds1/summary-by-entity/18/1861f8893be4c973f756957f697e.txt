Abstract It is proved that for a cosine family $${\\{c(t)\\}_{t \\in \\mathbb{R}}}$$ {c(t)}t∈R in a normed algebra with a unity e, the following assertions hold: (1) If $${\\sup_{t \\in \\mathbb{R}}\\| c(t) - e \\| < 2}$$ supt∈R‖c(t)-e‖<2 , then c(t) =  e for every $${t \\in \\mathbb{R}}$$ t∈R . (2) If $${\\lim sup_{t \\to 0}\\| c(t) - e \\| < 2}$$ limsupt→0‖c(t)-e‖<2 , then $${\\lim_{t \\to 0} c(t) = e}$$ limt→0c(t)=e . It is also shown that the two respective results, each specific for one of the assertions, are equivalent.

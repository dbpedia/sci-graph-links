Abstract We give a remarkable additional othogonality property of the classical Legendre polynomials on the real interval $$[-1,1]$$ [-1,1] : polynomials up to degree n from this family are mutually orthogonal under the arcsine measure weighted by the normalized degree-n Christoffel function.

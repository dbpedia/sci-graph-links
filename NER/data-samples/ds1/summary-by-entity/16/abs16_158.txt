Abstract We study the structures $$( F_\sigma \mathsf {ideals} , \le _{\mathrm {K}} )$$ (Fσideals,≤K) and $$( F_\sigma \mathsf {ideals} , \le _{\mathrm {KB}} )$$ (Fσideals,≤KB) , where $$F_\sigma \mathsf {ideals}$$ Fσideals is the family of all $$F_\sigma $$ Fσ -ideals over $$\omega $$ ω , and $$\le _{\mathrm {K}}$$ ≤K and $$\le _{\mathrm {KB}}$$ ≤KB denote the Katětov and Katětov-Blass orders on ideals. We prove the following: $$( F_\sigma \mathsf {ideals} , \le _{\mathrm {K}} )$$ (Fσideals,≤K) and $$( F_\sigma \mathsf {ideals} , \le _{\mathrm {KB}} )$$ (Fσideals,≤KB) are upward directed.The least cardinalities of cofinal subfamilies of $$( F_\sigma \mathsf {ideals} , \le _{\mathrm {K}} )$$ (Fσideals,≤K) and $$( F_\sigma \mathsf {ideals} , \le _{\mathrm {KB}} )$$ (Fσideals,≤KB) are both equal to $${\mathfrak {d}}$$ d . Moreover those of unbounded subfamilies are both equal to $${\mathfrak {b}}$$ b .The family of all summable ideals is unbounded in both $$( F_\sigma \mathsf {ideals} , \le _{\mathrm {K}} )$$ (Fσideals,≤K) and $$( F_\sigma \mathsf {ideals} , \le _{\mathrm {KB}} )$$ (Fσideals,≤KB) .

Abstract Digital library has begun to enter a new era of cloud computing after Internet era, Web era, Grid era and Web 2.0 era. Combined with cloud computing technology, this thesis constructed a cloud service platform of digital library and described the service process and operating mechanism of digital library cloud service platform.

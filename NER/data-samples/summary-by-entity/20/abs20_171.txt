Abstract There is a scholarly debate over whether there is a crisis among young men. This article uses portrayals of young men in popular culture as a barometer of how the wider society views the role of men. It maintains that positive images of young men have almost disappeared from popular cultures. This lack of role models has the unintended consequence of confusing and disheartening young men.

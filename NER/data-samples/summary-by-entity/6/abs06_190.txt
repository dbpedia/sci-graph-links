Abstract A new species of Trieces Townes from Brazil, Trieces tyloidalis n. sp., is described and illustrated. This is the first known species of Trieces with tyloids in the male antennae. The genus is recorded from Belize, Ecuador and Venezuela for the first time. Trieces horisme Gauld

Abstract With the financial support from International League of Associations for Rheumatology (ILAR), we held a rheumatologic care education project in Xishuang Ban’na, a remote district in southern China. During 2014–2015, our project devoted to improve rheumatologic care skills of local clinical practitioners and basic rheumatologic knowledge tothe public. This paper summarizes the undeveloped conditions of rheumatology and our effort in Xishuang Ban’na.

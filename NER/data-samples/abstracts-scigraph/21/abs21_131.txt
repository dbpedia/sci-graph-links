Abstract Every simple graphG=(V, E) can be represented by a family of equal nonoverlapping spheres {S v :v ∈ V} in a Euclidean spaceR n in such a way thatuv ∈ E if and only ifS u andS v touch each other. The smallest dimensionn needed to representG in such a way is called the contact dimension ofG and it is denoted by cd(G).

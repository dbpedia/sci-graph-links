Abstract In this paper we introduce the concept of $$\xi $$ ξ -torsion module, $$\xi $$ ξ -torsion-free module and $$\xi $$ ξ -torsionable module. We investigate many properties of these modules. We characterize $$\xi $$ ξ -torsion modules and $$\xi $$ ξ -torsion-free modules using short exact sequences and module homomorphisms.

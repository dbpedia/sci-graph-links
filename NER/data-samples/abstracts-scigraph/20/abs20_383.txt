Abstract The 9th Asian Symposium on Visualization (9ASV) was successfully held in Hong Kong from 4th to 8th June 2007. The total number of 191 participants from 14 countries/regions attended the Symposium, of which 174 were foreign participants worldwide outside Hong Kong.

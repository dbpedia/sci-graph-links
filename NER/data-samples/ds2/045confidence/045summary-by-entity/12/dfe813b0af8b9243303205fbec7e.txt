Abstract A construction based on a $$4l \\times 4l$$ 4l×4l Hadamard matrix leads to a new family of optimal orthoplex packings in Grassmannian spaces $$G_{\\mathbb {R}}(8l, 4l)$$ GR(8l,4l) and $$G_{\\mathbb {C}}(4l, 2l)$$ GC(4l,2l) . A related construction gives an optimal simplex packings in $$G_{\\mathbb {R}}(8 l-1, 4 l - 1)$$ GR(8l-1,4l-1) and $$G_{\\mathbb {R}}(8l-1, 4l)$$ GR(8l-1,4l) with the additional assumption of an $$8l \\times 8l$$ 8l×8l skew Hadamard matrix and a related 1-factorization of a complete graph. A construction of a maximal optimal simplex packings in $$G_{\\mathbb {C}}(2l-1, l- 1)$$ GC(2l-1,l-1) and $$G_{\\mathbb {C}}(2l-1,l)$$ GC(2l-1,l) is given.

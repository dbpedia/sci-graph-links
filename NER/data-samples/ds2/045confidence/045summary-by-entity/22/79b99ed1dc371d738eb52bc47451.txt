Abstract  This article describes the views of IRMM on accreditation of CRM producers and the philosophy which is behind these views. More specifically, it explains the reasons for choosing ISO Guide 34 rather than ISO/IEC 17025 as the basis document for the proposed accreditation of its CRM activites.

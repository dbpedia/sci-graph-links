Summary A rare alpha1-antitrypsin phenotype was detected in serum of individuals belonging to three generations of an American Negro family. Following the recommended guidelines of nomenclature, we labeled the new Pi type Zpratt; the corresponding allele is Pi Zpratt . Alpha1-antitrypsin concentration and trypsin inhibitory capacity are normal in MZpratt serum.

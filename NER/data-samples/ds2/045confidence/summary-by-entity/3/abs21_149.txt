Abstract To describe clinical and epidemiological data of Behçet’s disease (BD) in Brazil, we retrospectively reviewed records of all patients seen between 2006 and 2007 at the BD outpatient clinic of University of Sao Paulo. One hundred and six patients fulfilled the International Study Group for Behçet’s Disease diagnostic criteria and they were included in this study.

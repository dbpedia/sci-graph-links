To increase communication security, the transmitted message is encrypted with the techniques of N-shift cipher and public key. Based on Lyapunov method and linear matrix inequality (LMI) formulation, new delay-dependent synchronization criteria are established to not only guarantee stable synchronization of both transmitter and receiver systems but also recover the transmitted original signal at the receiver.

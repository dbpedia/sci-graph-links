Abstract We present high and low dispersion UV spectra of HDE 245770, which is the optical counterpart of the recurrent transient X-ray pulsar A 0535 26,during a decay of an X-ray flare. UBV photometric measurements and medium dispersion optical spectra were simultaneously obtained. In this phase, HDE 245770 does not show evaluable variations with respect to the quiescent X-ray phase.

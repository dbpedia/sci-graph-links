Summary For compact sets M ⊂ R2, investigating the problem of representing functions V: R2→ R+ in the form V(x)=ϱ(x, M), where ϱ is a suitable metric on R2, a characterisation of retracts is obtained. As for applications, two results in topological dynamics are given.

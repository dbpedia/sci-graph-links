Summary LetX i,n′ i=1, ...n be a triangular array in the domain of attraction of a Lévy processX(t). Then the symmetric polynomial of orderk in theX i,n′ $$\sum\limits_{1 \leqq i_1< i_2< \ldots< i_k \leqq [nt]} {X_{i_1 ,n} X_{i_2 ,n} \ldots X_{i_k ,n} ,}$$ converges weakly to thek th order multiple integral with respect toX(t). Also, sums of powers of theX i,n converge jointly to the variations of the processX(t).

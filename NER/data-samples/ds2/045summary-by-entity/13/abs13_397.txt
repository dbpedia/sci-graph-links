Abstract Swedish literature includes many novelsabout school experiences. These novels help usunderstand how teachers experienced changes inthe Swedish school system during the previouscentury. The most interesting novels are thosewritten by professional teachers. In thatrespect it is possible to connect this work tolife history research, an established field ineducational research for examining how privateindividuals perceived and related to events inhistory. This article I based on a researchproject entitled: “Educational work in theprism of the novel”.

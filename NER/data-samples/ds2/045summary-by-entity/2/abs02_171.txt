Abstract We introduce an amalgam type space, a subspace of $$L^1(\mathbf R_+)$$ L1(R+) . Integrability results for the Fourier transform of a function with the derivative from such an amalgam space are proved. As an application, we obtain conditions for the integrability of trigonometric series.

Abstract On the 24th June 2015, Feminist Legal Studies and the London School of Economics (LSE) Law Department hosted an afternoon event with Professor Wendy Brown, Class of 1936 First Professor of Political Science, University of California. Professor Brown kindly agreed to discuss her scholarship on feminist theory, and its relationship to both the law and neoliberalism.

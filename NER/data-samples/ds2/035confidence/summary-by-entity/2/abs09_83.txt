Abstract The Materials Center Leoben (MCL) is a Competence Center in the field of “Integrated Research in Materials, Process, and Product Engineering”. The MCL provides the manufacturing industry with advanced fundamentals, simulation methods, and experimental techniques in order to promote materials-enabled innovations and to strengthen competitiveness of this sector.

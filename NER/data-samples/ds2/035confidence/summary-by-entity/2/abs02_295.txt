Summary The time-resolved luminescence spectrum of AgBr excited with an intense pulsed UV laser is reported. A new emission line is attributed to the recombination of excitonic molecules. From the line shape analysis the biexciton radiusa M≃20 Å and the binding energyE B≃6.7 meV are deduced.

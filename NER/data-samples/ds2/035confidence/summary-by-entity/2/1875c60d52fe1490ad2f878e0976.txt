Abstract IfP is an elliptic self-adjoint perturbation of the Laplacian Δ on ℝ3, and the coefficients ofP−Δ decay super-exponentially, then we show thatP has infinitely many resonances. The resonances are defined here as the poles of the meromorphic continuation of (P−λ2)−1.

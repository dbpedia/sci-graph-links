Abstract The dominant reactions in semiconductor gas sensors are studied with the aid of five commercial thick-layer Taguchi gas sensors (TGS) manufactured by Figaro Inc. (Japan). The gas used in the tests was carbon monoxide in concentrations of 10 to 500 ppm.

Abstract This paper outlines the strategy and issues for the commercialisation of HARMST-LIGA process technologies. Part of this strategy is the setting up of an international HARMST-LIGA Commercialisation Group to identify and bring together people and organisations to advance industrial up-take and drive development and manufacturing of new product for the growing global markets.

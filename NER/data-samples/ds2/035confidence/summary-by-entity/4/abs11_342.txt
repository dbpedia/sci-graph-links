Summary Retinal Degeneration Processes are often based on a dysfunctional Retinal Pigment Epithelium (RPE), leading to difficulties in visual perception and finally resulting in practical blindness. In the last decade much research has been performed to find a cure or at least an arrest of progression of diseases as age-related macula degeneration (AMD). This article summarizes different research foci of the past, the present and future trends in retinal transplantation. In addition it contains the newest results of our basic research facility.

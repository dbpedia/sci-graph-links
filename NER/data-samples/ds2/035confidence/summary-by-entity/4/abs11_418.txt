In rats with experimental chronic glaucoma neuropathy, we studied the amounts of neurotransmitter amino acids in the vitreous body of the eye. Significant changes in the amino acid contents and imbalance between excitatory and inhibitory transmitter amino acids (a shift toward the predominance of excitatory ones) were found.

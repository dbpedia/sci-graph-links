Summary This paper is an attempt towards determination of station adjustments for Shillong and Delly Observatories from considerations of a large number ofP n residuals. Station adjustments toJeffreys-Bullen travel time tables for Shillong comes to about 4 seconds and for Delhi 2 seconds respectively for Δ≧20°.

Abstract Let R be a prime ring and L a noncommutative Lie ideal of R. Suppose that f is a right generalized β-derivation of R associated with a β-derivation δ such that f(x) n  = 0 for all $${x\in L}$$, where n is a fixed positive integer. Then f = 0.

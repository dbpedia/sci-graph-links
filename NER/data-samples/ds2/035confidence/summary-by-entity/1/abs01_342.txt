Abstract We introduce and study new distribution spaces, the test function space $${\mathcal {D}}_E$$ DE and its strong dual $${\mathcal {D}}'_{E'_{*}}$$ DE∗′′ . These spaces generalize the Schwartz spaces $${\mathcal {D}}_{L^{q}}$$ DLq , $${\mathcal {D}}'_{L^{p}}$$ DLp′ , $${\mathcal {B}}'$$ B′ and their weighted versions. The construction of our new distribution spaces is based on the analysis of a suitable translation-invariant Banach space of distributions $$E$$ E , which turns out to be a convolution module over a Beurling algebra $$L^{1}_{\omega }$$ Lω1 . The Banach space $$E'_{*}$$ E∗′ stands for $$L_{\check{\omega }}^1*E'$$ Lωˇ1∗E′ . We also study convolution and multiplicative products on $${\mathcal {D}}'_{E'_{*}}$$ DE∗′′ .

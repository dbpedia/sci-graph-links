Abstract. We study the radiative decays $\\omega\\rightarrow \\eta\\pi^{0}\\gamma$ and $\\rho^{0}\\rightarrow \\eta\\pi^{0}\\gamma$ within the framework of a phenomenological approach in which the contributions of the intermediate $a_0$-meson and intermediate vector meson states are considered. We conclude that although the $a_0$-meson intermediate state amplitude makes a small contribution to $\\omega\\rightarrow \\eta\\pi^{0}\\gamma$ decay, it makes a substantial contribution to $\\rho^0\\rightarrow \\eta\\pi^{0}\\gamma$ decay.

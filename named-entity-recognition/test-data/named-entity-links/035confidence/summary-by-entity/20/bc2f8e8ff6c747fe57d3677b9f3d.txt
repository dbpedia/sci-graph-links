Abstract We find the minimum dilatation of pseudo-Anosov braids on n-punctured discs for 3 ≤ n ≤ 8. This covers the results of Song-Ko-Los (n = 4) and Ham-Song (n = 5). The proof is elementary, and uses the Lefschetz formula.

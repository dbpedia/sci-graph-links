Summary In this note we estimate the rate of convergence in Marcinkiewicz-Zygmung strong law, for partial sumsS n of strong stationary mixing sequences of random variables. The results improve the corresponding ones obtained by Tze Leung Lai (1977) and Christian Hipp (1979).

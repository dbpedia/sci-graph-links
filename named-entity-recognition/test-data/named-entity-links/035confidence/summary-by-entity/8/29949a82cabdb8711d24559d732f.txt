Abstract A class of Quasi-Systematic Block-Circulant Low-Density Parity-Check (QSBC-LDPC) codes is proposed. Block-circulant LDPC codes have been studied a lot recently, because the simple structures of their parity-check matrices are very helpful to reduce the implementation complexities. QSBC-LDPC codes are special block-circulant LDPC codes with quasi-systematic parity-check matrices. The memories for encoders of QSBC-LDPC codes are limited, and the encoding process can be carried out in a simple recursive way with low complexities. Researches show that the QSBC-LDPC codes can provide remarkable performances with low encoding complexities.

Abstract We show that the automorphism group of a divisible design $${\\mathcal{D}}$$ is isomorphic to a subgroup H of index 1 or 2 in the automorphism group $${({\\mathcal{D}})}$$ of the associated constant weight code. Only in very special cases H is not the full automorphism group.

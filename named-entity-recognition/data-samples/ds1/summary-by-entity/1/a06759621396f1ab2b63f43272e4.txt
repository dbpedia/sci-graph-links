The paper considers some modern problems arising in developing parallel algorithms for solving large systems of linear algebraic equations with sparse matrices occurring in mathematical modeling of real-life processes and phenomena on a multiprocessor computer system (MCS). Two main requirements to methods and technologies under consideration are fast convergence of iterations and scalable parallelism, which are intrinsically contradictory and need a special investigation.

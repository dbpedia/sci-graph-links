Abstract Let P1 be the class of all finite groups that are products of two normal supersoluble subgroups. Let P be the class of all nonsupersoluble P1-groups G such that all proper P1-subgroups of G and nontrivial factor groups of G are supersoluble. We classify the P-groups.

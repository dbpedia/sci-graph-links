Abstract The leading twist contribution to χ c0,2 → ωω decays in the color-singlet approximation is considered. It is shown that the prediction for Br(χ c0 → ωω) is in good agreement with the experimental data, while Br(χ c2 → ωω) differs from the experiment significantly.
